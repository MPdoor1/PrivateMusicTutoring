Frequently Asked Questions

What types of lessons do you offer?

+

We offer private lessons for piano, guitar, violin, drums, and various other instruments. We also provide music theory, performance coaching, and music history lessons.

Can you teach beginners?

+

Absolutely! We have experienced instructors who specialize in teaching students of all ages and skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced players.

How do I get started?

+

Simply click the "Book Lesson" button on our homepage, fill out the booking form, and select your preferred instrument, lesson time, and skill level. We'll send you a confirmation email with your Zoom meeting details.

What are your rates?

+

Our rates vary by instrument and lesson type. For a general estimate, private piano lessons are $30 per 30-minute session, while guitar lessons are $40 per 45-minute session. Please contact us for specific rates and availability.

Do you offer in-person lessons?

+

Yes, we offer both online and in-person lessons. Our online platform is secure and reliable, while in-person lessons can be arranged in the Tampa Bay area.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

+

We require a 24-hour notice for cancellations to avoid being charged for the lesson. For reschedules, we can accommodate as long as the new time is available.
