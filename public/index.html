Frequently Asked Questions

What types of lessons do you offer?

+

I teach private lessons for EVERY instrument - piano, guitar, violin, drums, saxophone, flute, trumpet, bass, ukulele, and any instrument you can think of! Plus music theory, performance coaching, and music business. Every lesson is $40 - you're investing in musical growth and confidence building.

Can you teach beginners?

+

Absolutely! I specialize in teaching students of all ages and skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced players. Each lesson is tailored to your learning pace and goals - you're investing in musical development, not just time.

How do I get started?

+

Simply click the "Book Lesson" button, fill out the form, and choose your preferred instrument and lesson format (online via WhatsApp video call or in-person). Each lesson is 30 minutes for $40.

What are your rates?

+

Every lesson is $40 - you're investing in your child's musical development and confidence building, not paying for time on the clock. I teach every instrument and tailor each lesson to your child's unique learning style and goals.

Do you offer in-person lessons?

+

Yes! I offer both online lessons (via WhatsApp video call) and in-person lessons where I drive to your location in the Jacksonville, Florida area. All lessons are 30 minutes for $40.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

+

All lesson purchases are final - no cancellations, rescheduling, or refunds. As a professional contractor, I plan my schedule and personal commitments around the time you have set an expectation for. When these expectations are not met, it creates significant inconvenience both professionally and personally. Please be certain of your availability before booking.

What is your refund policy?

+

All lesson purchases are final and non-refundable. This ensures I can maintain consistent scheduling and provide the best service to all students. Please be certain of your commitment before booking.

What area do you serve for in-person lessons?

+

I provide in-person lessons throughout the Jacksonville, Florida area. I'll drive to your location for the convenience of learning in your own home. Online lessons via WhatsApp video call are available worldwide.
